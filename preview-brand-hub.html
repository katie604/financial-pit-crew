About Katie

Her story.

I've spent the last CONFIRM 5+ years working exclusively with trades and construction business owners across Australia and New Zealand. I kept seeing the same pattern: great tradies, terrible cash flow. So I built Profit First for Tradies around teaching the system to the people who need it most.

As a 4th-generation business owner who watched my parents run their company through the highs, the lows and some serious health stuff, I bring lived experience and zero jargon to every stage, podcast and coaching room I walk into.
