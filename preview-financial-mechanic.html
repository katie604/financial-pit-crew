The method we build on

Profit First

A simple cash-management system: you take your profit first, before the bills - so your business is profitable by design, not whatever happens to be left over (which is usually nothing). If you're winning work but the bank account never grows, this is the system that closes the gap.

Katie is a Certified Profit First Professional and the author of Profit First for Tradies - so you're learning it from someone who lives it and teaches it.
